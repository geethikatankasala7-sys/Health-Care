Emergency Health Tips

❤️ Heart Attack

Call emergency services immediately. Keep the patient calm.

🩸 Bleeding

Apply pressure to the wound using a clean cloth until help arrives.

🔥 Burns

Cool the burn with clean running water for 10–20 minutes.

🦴 Fracture

Do not move the injured area. Seek medical help immediately.
